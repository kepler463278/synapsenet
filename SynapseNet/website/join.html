Join the Network

Become part of the global mind in 5 simple steps

Step 1: Download the Node

Visit the download page and get the desktop application for your operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux).

The download is ~100MB and installation takes less than 5 minutes.

Step 2: Install and Launch

Follow the installation instructions for your platform:

  • Windows: Run the .exe installer
  • macOS: Drag to Applications folder
  • Linux: Make executable and run the AppImage

Launch SynapseNet from your applications menu or desktop shortcut.

Step 3: Start Your Node

When you first open SynapseNet, you'll see the home screen with a "Start Node" button.

  1. Click "Start Node"
  2. Wait for the node to connect to the network (usually 30-60 seconds)
  3. You'll see your peer count increase as you connect to other nodes

That's it! You're now part of the decentralized network.

Step 4: Add Your First Grain

Share your first piece of knowledge with the network:

  1. Go to the "Add" tab
  2. Enter some knowledge, insight, or idea
  3. Click "Add to Network"
  4. Watch as your grain is broadcast to peers
  5. See your first NGT reward!

Your contribution is now part of the collective intelligence.

Step 5: Explore and Earn

Now you can:

  • Search: Find knowledge across the network
  • Contribute: Add more grains and earn NGT
  • Validate: Help confirm valuable contributions
  • Participate: Join swarms for collective intelligence

What Happens Next?

As you use SynapseNet:

  • Your node will sync with the network automatically
  • You'll earn NGT for valuable contributions
  • Your grains will be validated by peers
  • You'll participate in emergent intelligence

You can start and stop your node anytime. When offline, you can still:

  • Search your local grains
  • View your reward history
  • Check your NGT balance

Tips for New Users

Maximize Your Rewards

  • Add novel, unique knowledge (higher novelty = higher rewards)
  • Contribute regularly (consistent participation is rewarded)
  • Validate peer contributions (help strengthen the network)
  • Participate in swarms (collective intelligence earns more)

Best Practices

  • Keep your node running when possible (helps the network)
  • Back up your data directory regularly
  • Add meaningful, valuable knowledge
  • Respect the network and other participants

Privacy and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • You choose what to share with the network
  • No personal information is required
  • Participation is anonymous by default

Understanding NGT Rewards

Network Growth Tokens (NGT) are earned through Proof-of-Emergence:

  • Novel Grains: 0.1-1.0 NGT based on novelty
  • Validations: 0.01-0.1 NGT per validation
  • Query Answers: 0.05-0.5 NGT per answer
  • Swarm Consensus: 0.1-2.0 NGT per participation

Remember: NGT cannot be bought or sold. It can only be earned through contribution.
