V. THE TECHNOLOGY

SynapseNet operates on three core mechanisms:

1. GRAINS: Units of Knowledge
   
   A grain is a quantum of knowledge—an insight, a fact, an idea.
   Grains flow through the network like synapses fire in the brain.
   Each grain carries:
   - Content: The knowledge itself
   - Context: Where it came from
   - Confidence: How much the network trusts it
   - Connections: What it relates to

   Grains are not documents. They are not files. They are living knowledge.

2. SWARMS: Collective Intelligence
   
   When a question arises, nodes form a swarm—a temporary collective intelligence.
   The swarm:
   - Searches for relevant grains
   - Evaluates their confidence
   - Synthesizes an answer
   - Reaches consensus
   - Dissolves when done

   Swarms are not committees. They are not algorithms. They are emergent intelligence.

3. PROOF-OF-EMERGENCE: Recognition of Value
   
   Contribution is rewarded through Proof-of-Emergence (PoE).
   The network recognizes value through:
   - Novelty: Is this knowledge new?
   - Utility: Does this knowledge help?
   - Validation: Do peers confirm this?
   - Participation: Are you contributing?

   PoE is not mining. It is not staking. It is recognition.

VI. THE ECONOMICS

Contribution is rewarded through NGT (Network Growth Tokens).

HOW YOU EARN:

• Add valuable knowledge → earn NGT
  The more novel your contribution, the more you earn.
  Novelty is measured by semantic distance from existing knowledge.

• Validate peer contributions → earn NGT
  Help the network distinguish signal from noise.
  Your validation strengthens collective intelligence.

• Answer queries → earn NGT
  When your knowledge helps answer a question, you earn.
  The network rewards those who contribute to solutions.

• Participate in consensus → earn NGT
  Join swarms, evaluate grains, reach agreement.
  Collective intelligence requires collective participation.

IMPORTANT PRINCIPLES:

This is not speculation. This is recognition of value created.
This is not a token sale. There is no ICO, no pre-mine, no allocation.
This is not blockchain. It's pure peer-to-peer with local-first storage.

NGT has no price because it has no market.
NGT has value because it represents contribution.
NGT grows the network because it rewards participation.

You cannot buy NGT. You can only earn it.
You cannot trade NGT. You can only use it.
You cannot hoard NGT. You can only contribute.
